About

Whether we have time or not, whether we have bookstores nearby or not, those of us who read do so because we are looking for answers to questions we didn't even know we needed.
Someone once said that reading allowed a person to have more than one life, because you lived the stories you read. At Lux Library we believe this to be true, we read because we believe things can be improved. A book is a great teacher and a clear example that by putting one word after another you can build a wonderful work. Maybe if we Colombians get together and read one book after another, we can build a better place for everyone.
It's possible. Anything is possible when you have a book at hand. Reading is a good method of escape, fun and culture. It is a tool that we have very close at hand and that allows us to educate our minds while giving us different perspectives and images that contribute to nourishing our intellect. And as if that were not enough, reading also relaxes and distracts us from everyday life and its problems, absorbing us into a parallel world full of stories, dramas, comedies or fantasies that sometimes even lead to a healthy addiction.
Reading educates and gives us a critical and analytical sense. Thanks to reading, we are able to discover different perspectives, we broaden our points of view and it allows us to access and enter unknown, parallel worlds, other realities, other thoughts and feelings.

There is no worse illiterate than the one who knowing how to read does not read
